Shipping & Delivery

Last updated: December 2024

1. Digital Delivery Overview

At PixelVault, we specialize in digital game delivery, which means you receive your games instantly without waiting for physical shipping. This page explains our digital delivery process and what you can expect after making a purchase.

2. Instant Digital Delivery

2.1 Immediate Access

Upon successful payment, you will receive immediate access to your purchased games:

  • Download links are sent to your email address
  • Games are added to your PixelVault library
  • No waiting time or shipping delays
  • Available 24/7 for download

2.2 Delivery Confirmation

You will receive the following confirmations:

  • Order Confirmation Email: Sent immediately after purchase
  • Download Instructions: Included in the confirmation email
  • Account Library Update: Games appear in your account instantly

3. Download Process

3.1 Download Methods

We provide multiple ways to access your purchased games:

  • Direct Download: Download files directly from our servers
  • Platform Integration: Direct activation for supported platforms
  • Account Library: Access through your PixelVault account
  • Email Links: Secure download links sent to your email

3.2 Download Requirements

To download your games, you need:

  • A stable internet connection
  • Sufficient storage space on your device
  • A valid PixelVault account
  • Compatible device and operating system

3.3 Download Speeds

Download speeds depend on:

  • Your internet connection speed
  • File size of the game
  • Server load and availability
  • Your geographic location

4. Platform-Specific Delivery

4.1 PC Games

For PC games, you will receive:

  • Direct download links for game files
  • Installation instructions
  • System requirement verification
  • Product keys or activation codes if required

4.2 Xbox Games

For Xbox games, you will receive:

  • Xbox Live redemption codes
  • Step-by-step activation instructions
  • Account linking guidance
  • Download queue management tips

4.3 Cross-Platform Games

For games available on multiple platforms:

  • Platform selection during purchase
  • Platform-specific download instructions
  • Compatibility information
  • Transfer options between platforms (where available)

5. Delivery Timeline

5.1 Standard Delivery

Immediate (0-5 minutes): Order confirmation and download access
Within 1 hour: Detailed download instructions email
24/7: Access to your game library

5.2 Pre-Order Delivery

For pre-ordered games:

  • Immediate confirmation of pre-order
  • Pre-load access when available (usually 24-48 hours before release)
  • Automatic delivery on release date
  • Early access notifications if applicable

6. Delivery Issues and Support

6.1 Common Issues

If you experience delivery issues:

  • Email Not Received: Check spam folder and contact support
  • Download Links Expired: Access games through your account library
  • Slow Downloads: Try different download times or contact support
  • Activation Problems: Verify system requirements and contact support

6.2 Technical Support

Our support team can help with:

  • Download troubleshooting
  • Installation assistance
  • Platform activation issues
  • Account access problems
  • System compatibility verification

7. Account and Library Management

7.1 Game Library

All purchased games are stored in your BeyondProgressivePlay.com account library:

  • Permanent access to all purchased games
  • Download history and re-download capability
  • Game updates and patches
  • Purchase history and receipts

7.2 Account Security

To protect your digital purchases:

  • Use strong, unique passwords
  • Enable two-factor authentication
  • Keep your email address updated
  • Monitor your account for unauthorized access

8. Regional Availability

8.1 Geographic Restrictions

Some games may have regional restrictions due to:

  • Publisher licensing agreements
  • Local content regulations
  • Platform availability
  • Language support limitations

8.2 International Delivery

We deliver to most countries worldwide:

  • Instant delivery regardless of location
  • Localized pricing where available
  • Multi-language support
  • Regional customer support

9. Gift Delivery

9.1 Gift Purchase Process

When purchasing games as gifts:

  • Provide recipient's email address during checkout
  • Gift recipient receives delivery email directly
  • Recipient can claim the gift through their account
  • Gift can be redeemed within 30 days of purchase

9.2 Gift Redemption

Gift recipients can:

  • Create a BeyondProgressivePlay.com account to claim the gift
  • Download the game immediately upon redemption
  • Access the game through their library
  • Receive support for installation and activation

10. Delivery Confirmation and Receipts

10.1 Order Confirmation

You will receive detailed order confirmations including:

  • Order number and transaction details
  • Game titles and platform information
  • Download instructions and links
  • Payment confirmation and receipt

10.2 Tax Documentation

For tax purposes, we provide:

  • Detailed receipts with tax information
  • Annual purchase summaries
  • Exportable transaction history
  • Tax documentation for business purchases
