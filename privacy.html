Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

At BeyondProgressivePlay.com, we collect information to provide better services to our customers and improve our gaming platform.

1.1 Personal Information

We may collect the following personal information:

  • Name and contact information (email address, phone number)
  • Billing and shipping addresses

  • Communication history with our support team

1.2 Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information about your use of our services:

  • Device information (IP address, browser type, operating system)
  • Usage patterns and preferences
  • Game library and purchase history
  • Website interaction data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To process orders, provide customer support, and deliver digital games
  • Account Management: To create and manage your account, verify your identity

  • Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve our services

  • Marketing: To send promotional offers (with your consent)

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following circumstances:

3.1 Service Providers

We work with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in:

  • Payment processing (Stripe, PayPal)

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure payment processing through certified providers
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
  • Secure data storage with encryption at rest

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

5.1 Access and Control

  • Access your personal information

5.2 Communication Preferences

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.
