Frequently Asked Questions

How do I download my purchased games?

+

After completing your purchase, you'll receive an email with download instructions. You can also access your games directly from your account dashboard.

What is your refund policy?

+

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all digital games. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, contact our support team for a full refund.

Do you support multiple platforms?

+

Yes! We offer games for both PC and Xbox platforms. Each game listing clearly shows which platforms are supported.

How secure is my payment information?

+

We use industry-standard SSL encryption and partner with secure payment processors like Stripe to ensure your payment information is always protected.

Can I gift games to friends?

+

Currently, we don't offer direct gifting, but you can purchase games and share the download links with friends. We're working on adding a gifting feature soon!
