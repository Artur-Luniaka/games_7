Return & Refund Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Overview

At PixelVault, we want you to be completely satisfied with your gaming experience. This policy outlines our return and refund procedures for digital games purchased through our platform.

2. 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all digital games purchased through PixelVault. This means you can request a refund within 30 days of your purchase date, subject to the conditions outlined below.

3. Refund Eligibility

3.1 Eligible for Refund

You may be eligible for a refund if:

  • You request the refund within 30 days of purchase
  • The game doesn't work on your system despite meeting minimum requirements
  • You accidentally purchased the wrong game
  • The game was purchased as a gift but the recipient already owns it
  • There are significant technical issues preventing gameplay
  • The game description was misleading or inaccurate

3.2 Not Eligible for Refund

Refunds will not be granted for:

  • Requests made after 30 days from purchase
  • Games that have been played for more than 2 hours
  • Games purchased during sales or promotions (unless defective)
  • DLC content or in-game purchases
  • Games that don't meet your personal preferences
  • System compatibility issues due to outdated hardware
  • Games purchased with promotional credits or gift cards

4. How to Request a Refund

4.1 Online Refund Request

To request a refund online:

  1. Log into your BeyondProgressivePlay.com account
  2. Go to "Order History" in your account dashboard
  3. Find the order you want to refund
  4. Click "Request Refund"
  5. Select a reason for the refund
  6. Provide additional details if required
  7. Submit your request

4.2 Contact Support

You can also request a refund by contacting our support team:

  • Email: refunds@BeyondProgressivePlay.com
  • Live Chat: Available 24/7 on our website
  • Phone: +61 2 9932 6051 (Mon-Fri 9AM-6PM AEST)

4.3 Required Information

When requesting a refund, please provide:

  • Order number or transaction ID
  • Game title and purchase date
  • Reason for refund request
  • Your account email address
  • Any relevant screenshots or error messages

5. Refund Processing

5.1 Review Process

All refund requests are reviewed by our support team within 1-3 business days. We may contact you for additional information if needed.

5.2 Approval and Processing

If your refund is approved:

  • You will receive an email confirmation
  • The game will be removed from your library
  • Refund will be processed to your original payment method
  • Processing time: 3-10 business days depending on your payment provider

5.3 Denial

If your refund request is denied, you will receive an email explaining the reason. You may appeal the decision by contacting our support team.

6. Refund Methods

6.1 Original Payment Method

Refunds are typically processed back to the original payment method used for the purchase:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 3-5 business days
  • PayPal: 1-3 business days
  • Digital Wallets: 1-2 business days

6.2 Store Credit

In some cases, we may offer store credit instead of a cash refund. Store credit:

  • Can be used for future purchases
  • Never expires
  • Cannot be converted to cash
  • Is available immediately upon approval

7. Special Circumstances

7.1 Defective Games

If a game is defective or has significant technical issues, we may offer a refund regardless of the 30-day limit or playtime restrictions.

7.2 Regional Restrictions

If a game is not available in your region due to publisher restrictions, we will provide a full refund.

7.3 Duplicate Purchases

If you accidentally purchase a game you already own, we will provide a refund upon verification.

8. Gift Purchases

For games purchased as gifts:

  • The gift recipient can request a refund if they already own the game
  • The original purchaser can request a refund if the gift was sent to the wrong person
  • Refunds for gifts are processed to the original purchaser's payment method

9. Bulk Purchases

For bulk purchases or bundle deals:

  • Individual games within a bundle may be refunded if they meet eligibility criteria
  • The refund amount will be prorated based on the bundle price
  • Bundle discounts may be forfeited if individual items are refunded

10. Dispute Resolution

If you disagree with a refund decision:

  1. Contact our support team to discuss the issue
  2. Provide additional evidence or clarification if needed
  3. Request escalation to a supervisor if necessary
  4. Consider mediation or arbitration for unresolved disputes
